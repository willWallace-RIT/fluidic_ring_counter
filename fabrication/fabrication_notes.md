# Fabrication Notes

## Materials
- PDMS (standard)
- Resin 3D printing (fast prototyping)
- Glass etching (high precision)

---

## Flow regime requirement

Re << 2000

---

## NOT gate structure

Each inverter requires:
- T-junction
- Bubble diversion channel
- Controlled pressure gradient

---

## Key constraints

- Prevent bubble merging
- Maintain laminar flow
- Ensure consistent surface wetting
- Delay is purely geometric
